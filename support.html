Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Au Pair?
An au pair is a young adult who travels abroad to live with a host family, providing childcare and cultural exchange. Learn more here.
How do I sign up for a course?
You can sign up by creating a profile and paying for the course online. Check our Course Registration page.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Details on payment options are available here.
How long does the training program last?
The standard program is 3 months, covering essential topics for au pairs and host families. See the Course Curriculum for details.
Can I change host families during my stay?
Yes, if the situation is unsuitable, we support safe and respectful host family changes. Learn about this process here.
What kind of support is available during the program?
We provide ongoing support including virtual classes, a community forum, and access to counselors. More info here.
Are the courses accredited or certified?
Our courses come with certificates of completion recognized by partner organizations. Details here.
How do host families apply?
Host families apply by registering online and completing a vetting process. More info here.
What safety measures are in place to prevent scams?
We verify all profiles and provide scam prevention training. Read more here.
Do I need experience to take this course?
No prior experience is needed; our courses are designed for beginners to advanced learners. Start here.
How can I contact BridgeAuPair for personalized help?
You can reach us via the contact form below or through our social media channels.
