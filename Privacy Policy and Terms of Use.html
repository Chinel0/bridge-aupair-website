The BridgeAuPair Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Hello. We are BridgeAuPair. Here's how we protect your data and respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we keep it safe.

Our role in your privacy

If you use our services, this policy applies to you. We act as a data controller responsible for your personal information.

Our responsibilities

We comply with data protection laws and take care to safeguard your personal data.

Your responsibilities

  • Read this Privacy Policy carefully.
  • Provide accurate information.
  • Keep your login credentials secure.
  • Notify us if your information changes.

When and how we collect data

We collect data when you register, make payments, or use our services.

Types of data we collect

This includes name, email, address, payment details, usage data, and cookies.

How and why we use your data

We use your data to provide services, communicate, and comply with legal requirements.

Your privacy choices and rights

You can access, update, or request deletion of your data. You can also control cookies.

How secure is the data we collect?

We use industry-standard security measures but cannot guarantee complete security.

Where do we store the data?

Your data is stored on secure servers located in Germany.

How long do we store your data?

We retain data only as long as necessary for service provision and legal compliance.

Third parties who process your data

We use trusted third-party services to operate our platform and payment processing.

How we use cookies

Cookies help us analyze traffic and improve your experience.

Making this policy great

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes.
