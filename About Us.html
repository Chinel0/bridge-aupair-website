About Us

BridgeAuPair was founded by Chinelo, who personally experienced the joys and challenges of being an au pair abroad. Inspired by this journey, the agency is dedicated to empowering au pairs and supporting host families with comprehensive training and ongoing assistance.

With a passion for cultural exchange and people, Chinelo created BridgeAuPair to build a trusted, safe, and supportive community for everyone involved.

Our mission is to create a trusted community where cultural exchange thrives, and every au pair and host family feels informed, secure, and supported throughout their experience.

What Makes Us Different

BridgeAuPair stands out through its unique 3-month training program designed to prepare au pairs and host families before placement. Our ongoing support and scam prevention initiatives ensure a safe and rewarding cultural exchange experience for everyone involved.
